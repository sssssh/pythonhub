- Universal Shortcuts
⌘ W  --  close windows
⌘ Q  --  quit apps
⌘ N  --  create a new window
⌘ M  --  minimize
⌘ H  --  hide apps
⌘ I  --  get info
⌘ A  --  select everything
⌘ S  --  Save
⌘ Z  --  Undo
⌘ ,  --  open an app's preferences


- System
⌘ Spacebar  --  spotlight
⌃ ⇧ Power or Eject  --  lock your mac
⌥ Volume  --  open sound settings
⌥ ⇧ Volume Up/Down  --  incremental volume and brightness adjustment
⌘ tab  --  fast app switching
